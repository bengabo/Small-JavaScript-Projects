Live Music Reviews

Slayer

Witching Hour (feat. Machine Head)

Live Intrusion

/

1995

Slayer's "Live Intrusion" is a concert video with a bit off stage antics thrown in between songs. "Witching Hour", one of the album’s real treats, is the cover of a Venom’s classic. The band is being joined onstage by Machine Head’s Robb Flynn and Chris Kontos. That performance is almost worth the price of admission alone.
